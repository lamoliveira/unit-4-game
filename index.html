Crystals Colectors instructions!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score. You win the game by matching the number to Achieve, you lose the game if your score goes above the number to Achieve. The value of each crystal is hidden from you, until you click on it for the first time.
